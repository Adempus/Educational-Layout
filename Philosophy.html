Four Blocks Lesson Plan

Read-Aloud \ Shared Reading
Purpose
To model fluent reading and to develop listening comprehension; reading motivation
Teacher
Read's a book to the student based on his or her interest.
Student
Listens to the story, then retells the story with as much detail as possible.
Time
10-15 minutes.
Readers Workshop
Purpose
To observe the student's miscues during oral reading.
Teacher
Records student's miscues after reading, then analyzes miscues with student using meaning, structure and visual cues.
Student
Reads a selection orally, (based on student's reading level and interests); analyzes miscues after reading with teacher.
Time
10-15 minutes.
Skills & Strategies Practice
Purpose
To make the student a more independent and strategic reader.
Teacher
Uses strategies and activities from Improving Reading to develop student's print, vocabulary or comprehension skills.
Student
Complete's activities assigned by teacher.
Time
10-15 minutes.
Writer's Workshop
Purpose
To work on better spelling, sentence structure, phonics and vocabulary skills.
Teacher
Asks the student to write several sentences about what they liked and learned during the less, helping the student as necessary.
Student
Writes about the day's lesson in complete sentences using correct punctuation; with the aid of a dictionary.
Time
10-15 minutes.
